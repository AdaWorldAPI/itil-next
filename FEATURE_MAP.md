- **Case** = Ticket (owned by agent)
- **Sub-case** = Escalation envelope (owned by specialist)
- **Task** = Smaller unit of work within a case

- Sub-case creation without losing case ownership
- Customer complete history visible to support analysts
- Entitlement lookup (SLA based on customer tier)
- Related case/resolution query (knowledge base)
- Manager visibility for monitoring (without taking over)

**What made it work:**
- Ownership was preserved by design
- Sub-cases were first-class entities with their own lifecycle
- Integration with billing meant full customer context
